Introduction

This plugin displays the most over and under represented facts in a patient cohort compared to a reference cohort (default = all patients).

Instructions

Navigate to the "Specify Data" tab. Then, drag and drop a Patient Set onto the "compare" input box. Optionally, you can also specify a "reference" patient set. Click the "View Results" tab to show a table of the 10 most over and under represented facts observed in the patient set. By default, the first and last ten facts are shown. You can enter a new "size" to change the number of facts shown at one time.
